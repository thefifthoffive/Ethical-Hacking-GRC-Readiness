# Ethical-Hacking-GRC-Readiness


## Executive Summary

Ethical hacking is only ethical when it is authorized, documented, and performed within a defined scope.

This repository contains three Governance, Risk, and Compliance (GRC) readiness projects completed for IT-157.

### Phase 1 – Legal Risk Brief
Research of the Computer Fraud and Abuse Act (CFAA) and Washington State cybercrime laws.

### Phase 2 – Engagement & Governance Kit
Creation of Rules of Engagement (RoE) and Permission to Test (PTT) documentation.

### Phase 3 – DoD Vulnerability Disclosure Policy Analysis
Analysis of the Department of Defense Vulnerability Disclosure Program and its Safe Harbor protections.
